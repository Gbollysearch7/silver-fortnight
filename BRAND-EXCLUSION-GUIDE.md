## üí° Pro Tips

### 1. **TradersYard Keywords = Separate List**
Don't exclude "tradersyard" or "traders yard"! These should be:
- Tracked separately
- Ranked for defensively
- Not part of Avalanche strategy (they're brand traffic)

### 2. **Competitor Comparison Keywords**
Keywords like "tradersyard vs ftmo" are tricky:
- They mention YOUR brand + competitor
- Keep if "tradersyard" is first
- Remove if competitor is first

### 3. **Generic Comparisons Are GOLD**
These are high-value:
- "prop firm vs broker"
- "funded account vs personal account"
- "challenge vs instant funding"
